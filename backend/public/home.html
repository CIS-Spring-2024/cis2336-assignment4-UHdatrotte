General Information

The Sweet Spot Cafeteria opened in Fall 2024 on the University of Houston Sugar Land Campus. The Sweet Spot is open to all UH students, faculty, and staff. Guests are allowed to be served with a slight upcharge. The Sweet Spot is a fast casual establishment that sereves customers hot and fresh premade meals with influence from various cultural cuisines.
